Neural Style Transfer

Neural Style Transfer is a process where the style from one image is taken and applied to the another image.

My model was based off the VGG19 model, a CNN which was first used to achvieve neural style transfer. Outputs from different layers of the CNN are used to represent style and content.

Two loss functions are used to measure the accuracy of our program. To learn more about it, check out the medium article or video explaining it.
