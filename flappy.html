Beating Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is really hard for humans. It requires precisely timed clicks, something that humans, especially myself, am not good at. One of my friends just got a crazy high score on it, and I want to crush. The problem: I suck at this game. The solution: Get AI to do it for me.

Step 1: Bring Flappy Bird into python. For this I just used pygame as it's the easiet way to make games in python. All the images I found online. This part is very straightfoward

I then used Neat, which is a library designed for NeuroEvolution. Since Flappy Bird is really simple, the AI picks it up in no time. After trial 50 it is able to get a score of over 1000. I think it's safe to say my friend won't be beating my score anymore.
