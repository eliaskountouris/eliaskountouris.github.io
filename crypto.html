AI Price Prediction

AI has long been used to predict the prices of stocks. With a recent spike in popularity for cryptocurencies, I wanted to try and make an AI to predict the prices of different coins like Bitcoin and Etherium.

My model is an RNN, or recurent neural network. You can check out my video and article for a more indepth look into how it works, but to quickly summarize, an RNN is used to look at data in a series. Things like music, stocks, language all have some sort of order to them. Stock prices, specifically, have a series of prices, with each having an associated time. When you feed in the data to the RNN, you give it not only a single data point, but the data from one time unit before and one time unit after it. This way the RNN is able to look at how the thing evovles over time.

Using these RNNs, a 96% accuracy was acheieved. In other words, 96% of the time my RNN was able to predict if the crypto currency price would go up or down. Of course, this was only tested on our training set. I do not personally advise using my model for investing. However, it does suggest that one day we might have neural networks capable of analuzing stocks and advising our investments.
