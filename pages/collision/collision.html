Elastic Collision Simulation

A 2d elastic collision simulator made with javscript that uses the spring force from a collision to find change in velocity.

Tutorial

Click on the screen to create a ball. Cycle through the balls at the bottom to view their properties. Pause the game to change the properties of the balls.
